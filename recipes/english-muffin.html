English Muffin

Description

An English muffin is a versatile breakfast bread known for its slightly crispy exterior and soft, chewy interior. It's typically round in shape, with a slightly cratered surface that's perfect for trapping melted butter or jam. Made from a yeast-based dough, English muffins are usually split open with a fork rather than sliced, resulting in a rustic texture that's ideal for toasting. They boast a delightful balance of tanginess and subtle sweetness, making them a beloved choice for morning meals or as a base for sandwiches any time of day.

Ingredients

  • English Muffin
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Two Eggs

Steps

  1. Toast English Muffin
  2. Fry the eggs
  3. Cook the ham
  4. Assemble the muffin and place cheese on top of eggs
